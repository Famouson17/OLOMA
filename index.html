Our Story

A Community with Deep Roots

Oloma is a community located in Akoko-Edo Local Government Area of Edo State, Nigeria. Situated in the northern part of Edo State, Oloma is part of the Akoko-Edo people — a proud and culturally rich group known for their traditions, warmth, and resilience.

The community has a long and distinguished history passed down through generations of elders, storytellers, and leaders. Our people have preserved their identity through language, festivals, and a deep connection to the land.

Today, Oloma looks forward with ambition — nurturing its youth, celebrating its heritage, and welcoming visitors and members of the diaspora who wish to reconnect with their roots.
